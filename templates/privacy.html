Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Spendly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that data.

Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage information — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the product.

We do not collect payment information. Spendly is free to use and does not process transactions.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and maintain your personal expense tracking dashboard.
  • Send you account-related communications (e.g. password resets).
  • Understand how the app is used so we can improve it.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts (hosted by Google) to display typography. Google may collect basic request data such as your IP address when fonts are loaded. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

We do not integrate any advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media trackers.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@spendly.app

We will do our best to respond within 5 business days.
